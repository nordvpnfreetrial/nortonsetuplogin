How to Enter your Norton product key?


To activate your Norton subscription, you need to Enter your Norton product key at norton.com/setup, a unique 25-character code provided with your purchase. Open the Norton application, locate the activation area, and input the product key accurately to unlock your subscription benefits.

Steps to Enter your Norton product key

  • Open the norton.com/setup application on your device.
  • Navigate to the "Enter your Norton product key" section.
  • Carefully type in your 25-character product key.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete activation.
